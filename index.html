THE ONLY NUTRITION GUIDE YOU'LL EVER NEED
We're not going to give you a 47-page meal plan you'll ignore. Here are the actual rules. Follow them and stop overthinking it.
The Rules
1
EAT REAL FOOD
If it came out of a factory and has 40 ingredients, put it back. Meat, fish, eggs, veg, fruit, rice, potatoes. That's your list. Done.
2
PROTEIN WITH EVERY MEAL
Chicken, beef, fish, eggs, Greek yogurt. Every. Single. Meal. Without it you're just doing the workouts for nothing. Don't waste your effort.
3
DRINK WATER. ACTUAL WATER.
Not Coke. Not juice. Not "sparkling water with natural flavours" which is just fancy Coke. Water. 2-3 litres a day. You'll feel like a different human being.
4
STOP EATING WHEN YOU'RE FULL
Revolutionary concept, we know. Your plate isn't a challenge. You don't get a medal for clearing it. Eat slowly, stop when full. That's it.
5
THE 80/20 RULE
Eat well 80% of the time. The other 20%? Live your life. Have the pizza. Have the pint. Just don't make it your entire personality.
